About the Event

World-Class Squash in Madrid

The Madrid Squash Open brings elite professional squash to the Spanish capital as an official PSA World Tour sanctioned event. Hosted by Club de Squash Tres Cantos — a club founded in 1996 with a proud tradition of developing squash in the Madrid region — the tournament is part of the Challenger Series, the proven pathway to the elite tier.

Witness the speed, power and skill of the world's best squash players up close. With the support of the Comunidad de Madrid, Ayuntamiento de Madrid and Ayuntamiento de Tres Cantos, this event marks a landmark moment for squash in Spain.

All matches will be broadcast live and free on YouTube — in English on SquashTV Live and in Spanish on the Club Squash Tres Cantos channel — reaching squash fans worldwide.
